Pizza Bagels

How good is this one, my personal favourite for quick lunchtime snag

These super easy pizza bagels make for an ultimate student snack and can be rustled up in just 3 minutes with a grand total of 3 steps. As well as the standard tomato and cheese, you can also chuck on your own toppings, although things like meats should be already cooked.

Ceebs expensive shop brought microwave pizzas aye

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Pasta/Pizza sauce
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Slice and toast the bagel to your desired crispyness.
  2. Top with the pasta sauce and cheese
  3. Microwave for 1-2 minutes until cheese has melted.
  4. Chow!
