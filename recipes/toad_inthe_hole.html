Toad in the Hole

A classic staple in british meals

An easy to follow and cheap recipe to make for students on that glorious student loan. Just like mum used to make it, in a little as 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Sausages 4
  • Plain flour 75 g
  • Eggs 2
  • Milk 200 ml
  • Oil 1 tsp

Steps

  1. In a baking dish, drizzle around the li inside.
  2. Add the sausages and cook in the oven at 200 degrees for 10 minutes.
  3. While the sausages cook, add the flour to a big bowl and whisk in the lik and eggs unli you’ve got a runny batter.
  4. Once sausages are partly done, remove from the oven and pour over the batter and return to bake for around 20-30 minute.
  5. Remove the finished dish once the batter has risen and is golden and crisp – keep an eye on it!
