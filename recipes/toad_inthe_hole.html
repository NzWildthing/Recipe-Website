Toad in the Hole

A classic staple in british meals

An easy to follow and cheap recipe to make for students on that glorious student loan. Just like mum used to make it, in a little as 20 minutes.

Ingredients

    Sausages 4 Plain flour 75 g Eggs 2 Milk 200 ml Oil 1 tsp

Steps

    In a baking dish, drizzle around the oil inside. Add the sausages and cook in the oven at 200 degrees for 10 minutes. While the sausages cook, add the flour to a big bowl and whisk in the milk and eggs until you’ve got a runny batter. Once sausages are partly done, remove from the oven and pour over the batter and return to bake for around 20-30 minute. Remove the finished dish once the batter has risen and is golden and crisp – keep an eye on it!
